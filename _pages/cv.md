# Professional Training

**Cultural Competence in Computing (3C) Fellows Program**, Duke University **2021-22**
A year long program from Duke University that helps computing faculty
learn more about DEI issues and develop course content or other
interventions in their department to foster more inclusive and equitable
environments.

**CSinParallel Virtual Summer Workshop** **2021**
A 2.5-day NSF-funded hands-on training workshop on OpenMP parallel
computing and MPI distributed computing on both Raspberry Pi and remote
high-performance systems and clusters, discussions about how to teach
parallel and distributed computing (PDC), an introduction to materials
for teaching "parallel thinking,".

**ToUCH Virtual Workshop on Teaching Heterogeneous Parallel Computing**
**2022**
A multi-day online workshop on teaching parallel computing on
heterogeneous systems, covering GPU programming with OpenMP and CUDA,
heterogeneous architectures, hybrid algorithms and task mapping and
scheduling on heterogeneous systems.
